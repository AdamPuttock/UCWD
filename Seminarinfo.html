Information regarding Seminars

What to expect from Seminars:

  • The oppertunity to engage with fellow studets on the topic
  • Complete set tasks with supervision from a seminar leader
  • Group work to improve team-working ability
  • Practical tasks to develop your knowledge in the subject

How do I approach Seminars?

You should be approaching seminars, first knowing what subject is going to be convered in the seminar. You should have the equipment that is required avaliable, for be prepared to complete the tasks that are set for you.

What should I take away from Seminars?

You should have a broader view of the information that is covered in lectures, with some practical practise also.
