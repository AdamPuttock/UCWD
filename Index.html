Semester Information

Some of the units are in either Semester 1 or Semester 2, however some units can fall into both. As follows are the units in Computing, and the Semester they are based in:

Semester
Business and Professional Issues: Semester 1
Relational Databases: Semester 1
Programming: Semester 1 and 2
User-Centered Web Development: Semester 1
System Analysis and Design: Semester 2
Computers and Networks: Semester 2

On this course you will usually be taught by a range of staff with relevant expertise and knowledge appropriate to the content of the unit. This will include senior academic staff, qualified professional practitioners, demonstrators, technicians and research students. You will also benefit from regular guest lectures from industry.
